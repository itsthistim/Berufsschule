koala was created to bring some utility features and quality-of-life improvements to Discord

See what koala has to offer

  • Image Manipulation to create your own funny images
  • A Music System to play songs to your friends in a call
  • Inspiring Quotes generated by InspiroBot to brighten up your day

Obviously this is not it! Many more features like browsing memes via Reddit, showing information and stats about you and the servers you are in and funny random facts or jokes are available. Furthemore there is many more features actively in development.
